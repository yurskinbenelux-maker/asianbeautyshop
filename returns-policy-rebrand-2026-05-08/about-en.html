Our story

Asian Beauty Shop was founded in 2026 by K'Elmus Group BV, a Belgian company based in Aartselaar near Antwerp, out of a single observation: Korea has spent two decades quietly leading global skincare innovation, but the European experience of actually buying it has been a mess.

Counterfeits sold as authentic. Ingredient lists missing or in the wrong alphabet. Three-week shipping from Asian warehouses. Customer service in a language you don't speak. No EU compliance verification — you find out the hard way when the parcel gets stuck at customs.

We started Asian Beauty Shop to fix that gap. A small, curated catalogue of K-beauty brands we believe in, fully EU-registered, stocked in Belgium, shipped within 24 hours, and supported in English, Dutch, French, and Russian.

The premise is simple: when the operational layer is right, the products speak for themselves.

Our mission

To make Korean skincare easy in Europe. Every brand we list is EU-compliant before it goes live. Every order ships fast from our Belgian hub. Every page is in your language. Every ingredient is fully disclosed. Every parcel has a real person behind it — not a chatbot, not a drop-shipper.

Our values

  • Curation over catalogue. A short list earns more trust than an endless one. Every brand and product on our shelves had to convince us first.
  • Authenticity. We source direct from manufacturers and authorised distributors. No grey market, no knockoffs, no parallel imports.
  • EU compliance, by default. Every product is CPNP-registered before it goes live. Ingredient labelling, claims, and safety files are verified to EU 1223/2009.
  • Transparency. Full INCI lists on every product, honest reviews, clear pricing, return policies that actually respect your rights under Belgian and EU consumer law.
  • European service. Stocked in Belgium, shipped via tracked European carriers, supported in four languages, billed in EUR with proper VAT invoicing.

How we choose what we carry

We don't manufacture our own products — we curate. That means every formula on Asian Beauty Shop has earned its spot by passing four checks before it ever appears on the storefront.

Formulation. We look for brands whose products are built around active ingredients with real published evidence — not seasonal trends. We read the INCI lists before we read the marketing.

Manufacturing. Every brand we carry is produced at a GMP-certified Korean facility with documented quality-control standards. Examples include leading Korean OEMs such as ECIS and CIT, used by our flagship partner Yu.R Skin Solution.

Compliance. Before listing, every SKU goes through CPNP notification. We don't take a brand's word for it — we verify the registration ourselves.

Operational fit. Brands that can supply us reliably, with EU-format ingredient declarations and a willingness to support EU consumer rights (returns, recalls, ingredient queries). Korean-market-only brands don't pass this filter.

Certifications & compliance

Asian Beauty Shop operates under Belgian and EU law. Our company, K'Elmus Group BV, is registered in Belgium under VAT BE 1031.312.116 and KBO/BCE number 1031.312.116.

Every product on the storefront is registered in the EU's Cosmetic Product Notification Portal (CPNP) before listing. The brands we carry typically hold further international certifications, including GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice), ECAS (Emirates Conformity Assessment Scheme), and Korea's KFDA registration.

If you ever want documentation for a specific product — CPNP reference, full ingredient declaration, or manufacturer GMP certificate — write to info@kelmusgroup.eu and we'll provide it.

A note on pregnancy & breastfeeding

The products we carry are formulated with carefully selected ingredients and are designed to be gentle on the skin. During pregnancy and breastfeeding, however, skin can become more sensitive — we advise consulting a healthcare professional before introducing any new skincare into your routine during this period. If you'd like a product-specific ingredient review, our team is happy to flag actives commonly avoided in pregnancy (retinoids, salicylic acid above 2%, etc.) — just ask.
